About

I'm a Full-Stack Software Engineer based in Dallas, Texas.


Previously I have dealt with the web development as an employee of the digital department of the advertising agency. I was involved in working both with the clients and the development department. On of my duties was composing technical specifications for websites and then checking them for running properly. This experience has provided me with understanding how the website should look like for customer and what would be the most user-friendly and responsive interface. At that time the web-development job seemed too complicated to me though.


After moving to the USA in 2021, I decided to challenge myself and dive into web programming.


Since April 2022, I’ve been studying web development through online courses and became a certified full-stack developer. My early websites weren’t perfect, but I dedicated countless hours to improving them and honing my skills. Now, I love creating visually appealing, responsive, and user-friendly websites and applications.
